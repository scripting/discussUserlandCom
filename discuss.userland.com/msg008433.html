Hello,

This is a minor update. ODB Extractor 1.1.3 fixes an installation problem which was introduced in 1.1.1, and which could occur when using Frontier 5.x. Thanks to J-C Bagneris, Charles Kippley, Omar Vasquez, and Jacob Lyles who reported the problem and tested the fix.

ODBE 1.1.3 can be downloaded from:

http://www.mose.com/flip/frontier/files/odbe.sit.hqx
http://www.mose.com/flip/frontier/files/odbe.zip

Or from the page at:

http://www.mose.com/flip/frontier/odb_extractor.html

ODBExtractor lets you browse from Frontier 5 or 6 a remote root (even a 4.2 or a corrupt one), and import its objects without launching it. It makes easy to migrate your work between roots, and to recover your data from a root which has been corrupted.
