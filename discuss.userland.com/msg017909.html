Well, if nobody is around I'm definitely not somebody important enough to meet for dinner alone :-)

I do live right in the area, so if you're looking for anything to do I can offer some suggestions:

  • Playland - This is a park in Rye (quick drive down 287) with a beach and rides and a pretty nice atmosphere.
  • Kensico Damn - located in Valhalla (my home town). I believe it's the biggets damn on the east coast. There's a nice area out in front to lounge, read, walk around. They usually do fireworks on the 4th there. A good place to see them.
  • Historic Hudson Valley - Plenty of hitsorical places like Sunnyside, Van Cortland and Philipsburg Manor to visit.

Or you could stop by my workplace and laugh at how far behind in technology we are ;-)
