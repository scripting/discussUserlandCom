Huun-Huur-Tu is an awesome Tuvan throat-singing group that loves to visit San Francisco... probably a fuller, more appreciative audience here!

Last year they were excellent in the Great American Music Hall – I sat 2 tables away from the stage and was delighted by their musical talent for hours on end.

Their tour schedule shows August 2nd, 2000 as their next visit to San Francisco, but they're about to play in the UK, then France, then on to the US.
