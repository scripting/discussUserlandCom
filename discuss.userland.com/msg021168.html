My pov on Napster is pretty complex. As a creative person, I see it from the pov of the artists. But I am also a user of music, and I see it from that pov as well. I think artists should be paid for their work. And I think that users should be able to control how they use the music they've paid for. I see the changes in the music industry as a consequence of the "softwareness" of music. It changed. The music industry didn't respond. The users eventually take control. You have to deal with that.
