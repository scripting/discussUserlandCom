I have no idea about an 'ESC facilitator', but a document camera is the video-camera version of the old overhead projectors - you plug into a video projector, slap on a paper document and away you go. We use them here at SCU in the larger lecture theatres where a big enough OHP would be too expensive.

Also, if you zoom in far enough on something interesting, you can use them to make 'abstract presentation art' while your laptop boots...
