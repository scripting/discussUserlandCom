Here would be my timeline, as best as I can recall. All the info is around and dated on the My.UserLand.Com site and in DaveNet.

1. Scripting News started syndicating in XML in format. (12/97)

2. Netscape introduced RSS 0.9 supported by My.Netscape.Com. (3/99)

3. My.UserLand.Com unveiled, supporting RSS 0.9. (4/99)

4. My.UserLand.Com supports 2.0b1. (5/99)

5. Netscape introduced RSS 0.91, incorporating most of the features of 2.0b1. (Date?)

6. My.UserLand.Com supports RSS 0.91. (Same time as #5.)
