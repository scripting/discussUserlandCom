Erik, there are (at least) two solutions:

If you plan to have all web serving handled by mainResponder, you can safely delete user.webserver.postFilters.commonLog. (The original lives at builtins.webserver.postFilters.commonLog, in case decide you do need it at a later point.)

Or you can create a filespec at user.webserver.prefs.logFile pointing to a text file on your hard drive.
