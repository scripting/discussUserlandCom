Yikes. Talk about not understanding the essence of technology. These fours points about XML are completely off base. Its never the technology that matters but the application of the technology. Thousands of superior technologies have been created, but that is never the issue. As long as it gets the job done, the technology is secondary. Beyond that its all marketing.

XML isn't making anything easier, its people agreeing to common formats that makes the difference.

  1. A better way to search.
  2. A better way to distribute and track information.
  3. A better way to do business.
  4. A better way to do business... on the road.
