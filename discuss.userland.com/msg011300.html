I remember working at Kings College (Cambridge University in the UK) in a little basement computer lab called 'The Turing Room', after the man himself.

Up on the wall were lots of photos of Alan Turing in very slightly different poses. All looking out at us working on these keyboards.

Kind of spooky, but also kind of nice.
