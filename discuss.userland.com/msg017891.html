That's a mystery for me.. :) What I can't figure out is where is that Internet company signing all the big names away from the record labels and offering them better deals? If its so bad for artists and consumers, why hasnt this happened yet? In other words, for all the bitching and moaning, where's the beef?

For how long do artists have to sign up with a label to receive a break-even zero dollars as Courtney Love computes? Something here sure does not add up.
