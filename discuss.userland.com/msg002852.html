If you take a look at source for http://psyberspace.net/hub/www/ you'll find some simple comments demarking days and news items.

With the upcoming release of a shared browser menu for the ocha news suite, this simple method would support the aggregation of news sites by a simple spider and parse routine.

Sure, for enterprise apps, you want the info abstracted and served via another port. But a lot of weblog publishers don't have their own servers (or the latest version of Frontier for that matter).

Suggestions to make this prettier or more effective would be welcome.
