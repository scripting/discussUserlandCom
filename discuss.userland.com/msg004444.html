It's interesting to see the "presentation paradigm" shift from VERTICAL to HORIZONTAL, like having multiple "slide shows" piled on top of another, or like a "book" where individual pages contain "books" of their own.

At the first sight, this seems to work *very* well; it's fun to walk the site this way, and it seems to be easier to find what you are looking for. Frankly, I have never been a friend of "left column navigation frames" or "left frame navigation columns". For some reasons, these tend to give me the impression of a "narrow" site...
