Of course. Frontier is all about interfaces. First it was Apple Events, and then XML-RPC. Open interfaces at both ends, with our stuff replaceable anywhere. Work with us on creating the interfaces that are needed. We're into frameworks that do useful things and open doors for new stuff created by other people. This is the essence of win-win, and also (IMHO) the essence of the web philosophy.
