I also switched from RedHat to Caldera. The easiest (and cheapest) way to get the Caldera distribution is to buy "The Complete Idiot's Guide to Linux" by Ricart. For $16.95 at Frys you get a decent book plus a CD with the Caldera software including StarOffice, a Works-like productivity package. As a non-technical person with an end-users point of view, I found the Caldera installation and KDE interface much nicer than Red Hat.

Now, if only someone would port MORE to Linux...
