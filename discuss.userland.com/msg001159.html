Announcing frontierMath

Author:Tom Clifton
Posted:12/13/1998; 3:03:10 PM
Topic:Frontier-related announcements
Msg #:1159 (In response to 1117)
Prev/Next:1158 / 1160

ES Designs is pleased to announce the availability of frontierMath 1.0b2. The frontierMath extension provides a cross-platform set of math functions to Frontier users. It provides the same functionality of the trigCMD and simpleMath UCMDs, but as a dll that will work with Frontier on both Windows and Mac environments.

The frontierMath extension was written for ES Designs by Arnold Lesikar, the developer of the simpleMath UCMD. The extension is being distributed under the GNU GENERAL PUBLIC LICENSE. You are free to download, use, and modify the extension to suit your needs providing you follow the stipulations in the license agreement.

frontierMath is available for download at: <http://www.es-designs.com/download/frontiermath.html>

Tom Clifton & Arnold Lesikar
