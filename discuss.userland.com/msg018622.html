It's a sad fact that ZDNet throws up these articles every couple of weeks to help increase their banner ad hits. The pattern's more or less the same each time: Find a high-volume website. I can back that one up. It's usually an anti-Linux troll, though, in the hope of provoking the Slashdot effect. But people are wising up, and browsing with a banner-blocking proxy such as Junkbuster. Sadly, it doesn't yet have the capability to filter out cheap journalism...

Consider it a backhanded compliment, if you can, that ZDNet considers you a big enough target to bring the hits in.
