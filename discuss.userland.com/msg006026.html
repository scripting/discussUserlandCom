It's worth mentioning that they showed a working technology demo of MRJ code based on 1.2(Java 2), while it seemed to be quite a bit faster than even Sun's Win32 implementation, they are focusing on the performance of their 1.1.x code right now. They see this as their priority since the larger portion of app developers using Java are in that space.

They also noted that they have been working directly with developers with working apps to make sure that they were getting the performance and stability they would expect. They think that this is far more important than benchmark optimization.
