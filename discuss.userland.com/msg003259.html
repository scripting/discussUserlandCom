Job Description: Responsible for development and implementation of site architecture and guidelines to create an integrated look and feel through-out site extending to new additions and redesigned features. Will work with cross-functional teams including marketing, sales, product management to create awareness and positioning of product. Collaborate with Website Administrator and WebMaster to implement intuitive navigation, corporate look and feel, and integrate marketing messages through-out a 3000 page website.

Must be skilled in creation of web-ready graphics, animations, pdfs and multimedia files. Firm grasp of the design principles involved in creating attractive pages and workable interfaces. Knowledge of state-of-the-art web design tools, such as Dreamweaver or Cyberstudio, as well as standard computer-based design tools such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. Knowledge of FileMaker Pro and Frontier a plus.

Education/Experience: Graphic Design, Industrial Design or equivalent experience with corporate websites. Resume should include URLs or online portfolio.

Contact: Cindy Solomon, Cindy_Solomon@filemaker.com.
