The latest issue of the German computer magazine c't includes a full page review of Frontier 6 (in German).

http://www.ix.de/ct/99/18/064/

A quick translation of the conclusion: New functions like the http server components and guest databases have expanded Frontier into the ideal tool for web-based workflow. Beginners will look in vain for printed documentation; the online documentation only partly makes up for this. However, the trouble will pay off: the UserLand web site, the excellent Frontier mailing lists as well as the online discussion forum answer all open questions.
