From Jim Whitehead, ejw@ics.uci.edu. Jim is the chair of the IETF WebDAV group.

You, and folks on scripting.com might be interested in Sharemation, the first DAV-capable Internet storage site. In under 5 minutes, you can be up and running with 20 Meg of storage, accessible via their Web interface, or through Web Folders in IE5 or Office 2000. It's the first of many such DAV-capable storage sites to come.

The site has a nice access control interface, allowing you to create subdirectories that are quota limited, and to share files among a group of collaborators (assuming they also have Sharemation accounts).
