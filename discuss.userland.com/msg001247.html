I've used Mops for like a few weeks, but I didn't like it much. I learned Forth on an IMC700 (a microcomputer) and basically that's still the box I use it on most. It's fun to work with digital and analog inputs and outputs in Forth. I'm still looking for a free/cheap text-only Forth (the way we know it :) for the Mac with TCP-support. There are lotsa Forths for the Mac, like this4th, PocketForth (VERY GOOD!), and many more. look at http://www.forth.org/compilers.html
