The question of who owns information is ofcourse not new. To those who are interested in the general question I can recommend

Who owns information? : from privacy to public access by Anne Wells Branscomb. New York, NY : BasicBooks (1994) ISBN: 0-465-09175-X

which although somewhat dated in some respects has a number of interesting cases.

A review from Law Technology Journal, Vol 4, No 2 can be found at http://www.law.warwick.ac.uk/ltj/4-2i.html (deep linking?)
