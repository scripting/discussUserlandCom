I noticed a mention of menu sharing at the bottom of Mozilla's latest Menu Spec; since shared menus are listed as "not a requirement", I'm not sure if Netscape plans to continue supporting menu sharing in 5.0.

Maybe that doesn't matter now that we have XML-RPC, but if you want menu sharing it might not hurt to let Netscape know.

Mozilla.org also has a bunch of new XUL stuff online that XML hackers might want to look at. (I see a lot of potential for building broadband DHTML applications...)
