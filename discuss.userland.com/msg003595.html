Yes and no.

We've had cable modems here since August 97 (we were beta testers), so we've seen a lot of growth in that time. If you have a reasonably intelligent operator they'll have budgeted to plan for this. Subscriber growth should lead to infrastructure growth.

And yes, our cable system (MediaOne) also has a similar silly subscriber agreement. However, we haven't exactly been subtle about the fact that we've networked everything together, and we've never heard a peep from them in the last 19 months. It's not as if they have the manpower to go door-to-door, looking to see who's running what inside their houses.
