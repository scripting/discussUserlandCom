http://www.macrobyteresources.com/scripting/frontier/tonto/

Tonto is a small helper application for Frontier. It puts up a small floating window. You can register scripts with the application, which places buttons for those scripts in the floating window. So, Tonto gives you easy, 'clickable' access to your scripts.

The floating window is available in all applications! Also, the buttons shown in the window can be global (all apps), context-sensitive (specific to the frontmost app), or a combination of both.

Version 1.0a3 is an alpha version, but it's stable.

Enjoy!
