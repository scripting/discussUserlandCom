Do the economics of producing and providing trailers only make sense if the ad banners get loaded?

Why limit the audience for a trailer? If more people see a trailer, presumably more people will want to see the movie. Universal then makes more money.

Movie-List isn't making copies of the trailers. Universal still gets to see how many people are viewing each trailer by counting their hits.

Movie-List is giving them free publicity.

I don't see the problem.
