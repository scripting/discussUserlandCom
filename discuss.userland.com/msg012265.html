Hunh, maybe I am missing something here, but I found it in three clicks without any difficulty:

If All Else Fails

After you have read the W3C Webmaster Frequently Asked Questions and determined that contacting the Webmaster is the only way to answer your question, send email to (address).

The buttons to press:

  • Display item 6 (I need technical assistance)
  • I need more information
  • Contact Webmaster

Voila! It seemed pretty straightforward to me, if needlessly complicated. Perhaps this is just a case of wierd menu selections?
