In my experience, the duplicates on this DG don't appear when you hit the Edit this page button, they appear when:

  • you start a new topic and enter your message (or reply to an existing message with the TEXTAREA below the message)
  • submit the new message
  • Hit the back button in your browser
  • edit the text (spelling mistake, etc)
  • submit the new message again.
