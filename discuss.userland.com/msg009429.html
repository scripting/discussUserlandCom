Deep linking and financial institutions

Author:Dave Winer

Topic:LaSalle's policy re deep linking

Prev/Next:9427 / 9430

Deep linking is a completely different issue for banks, brokerages, lending institutions, any site dealing with money and private information. I can understand why LaSalle wants an unambiguous restriction on linking, in case Joe starts passing around links into John's bank account. Sure, they need other forms of security too, but I can understand why they don't want the public jumping deep into a financial transaction.
