An article from Marc's TechVision column at netscape.com:

The Collabra Decision - December 31, 1996

"In the fall of 1995, Netscape faced a dilemma. We had recently acquired Collabra Software, a company that produced a new and already highly successful product called Collabra Share, which allowed people to communicate and work together in groups and teams. It was clear to us that Internet infrastructure and standards provided the catalyst for groupware to go from a niche solution to one that's as ubiquitous as email or the Web."
