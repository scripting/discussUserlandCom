If I understand correctly, there is no real formalized structure to deal with binaries other than images then?

Generally I can see two kinds of binaries. Ones that could be included in a page. Similarly to how images can be included in pages now. The second would be linking to binaries such as sounds and PDFs.

So maybe the thing for me to do is put out for folks to peruse, my binaryLink suite.
