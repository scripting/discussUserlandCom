In Seattle with USwest, the physical setup is really easy. You plug their cicso "modem" into the phone jack, and plug the ethernet cable from the dsl "modem" into your computer or a hub. You have to set a couple of settings in the modem aka router, but other than that it's pretty easy.

My guess is that the minimum requirements are for one of two things. Either they distribute netscape 4.5 or something similar on the bloatware scale as their software, or you need to use some specific login software to get on their system.
