This just came in the mail from Waggener-Edstrom, Microsoft's PR firm:


Press Briefing: Developing Web Applications

Microsoft's new Developer Division will hold a press briefing with Microsoft President Steve Ballmer and Group Vice President Paul Maritz on Monday, Sept. 13.

We'll be sending you an invitation in the next couple of weeks, but we wanted to give you an opportunity to mark your calendar and make appropriate travel arrangements.

Ballmer and Maritz will outline Microsoft's long-term vision for developers building the next generation of Web applications and discuss the exciting new products that will help them get there. They will make several announcements focused on how to build applications for the next millennium.

When: 8:30 a.m. Monday, Sept. 13
Where: Sheraton Palace Hotel
2 New Montgomery St.
San Francisco, CA 94105
