This is excellent news, especially given that WebObjects can now be Java-driven as well as Objective-C-driven.

WO has been Java-driven as well as Objective-C-driven for years now. They had a "Java Bridge" that allowed you to instanciate and (seamlessly) use Obj-C (OpenStep Foundation/EOF/WO) objects from Java.

The version 5 annoncement is that the whole of WO is being written in Java, and thus it will be Java only.

Apparently 4.5 will be supported for quite some time, however.
