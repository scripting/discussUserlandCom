CNET has a nice article, Web sites follow down to the wire election.

The article links to lots of interesting vote tracking sites.  However, I agree with the editor of netelection.org, who is quoted in the article:

Steven M. Schneider, editor of Netelection.org, which is sponsored by the Annenberg Public Policy Center at the University of Pennsylvania. "It's tough for Web sites to compete with television on Election Day when all there is is poll results."

"The only question that matters now is, Who won?" Schneider said. "Election Day belongs to TV."

Yup.  Gonna watch it on TV.
