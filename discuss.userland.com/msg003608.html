A few days ago I added a bunch of new tags to the "legaltags" table on this server. Here's a list of HTML tags that the DG will allow you to add to messages you post:

  • a
  • b
  • blockquote
  • i
  • img
  • li
  • ol
  • pre
  • table
  • td
  • tr
  • ul

Please use them with care and good judgement. Your words do more to add emphasis than markup commands will.
