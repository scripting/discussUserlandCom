A very common, and very incorrect, meme in this thread is that the GPL is "anti-commercial". It's not, it's anti-closed/proprietary.

Not all closed/proprietary source is commercial.

Not all commercial source is closed/proprietary.

Saying "the GPL is anti-commercial" is not correct.
