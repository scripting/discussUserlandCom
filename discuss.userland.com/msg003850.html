You can apply this to web/interface/interaction design in general. Via copy he conditions the user to look for a "specific card". Therefore, we are only paying attention to superficial detials of the other cards -- total number of cards, number/color or individual cards. The only number/suit combination you are focusing on is "your card". Therefore, you may not notice that a nine of hearts has become a nine of diamonds.

Clever use of mis-direction.

It is easier to undercover the solution if 2 people attempt to do it at the same time.

Try it.
