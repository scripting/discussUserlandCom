Can't remember you password? Maybe your browser should remember it for you! I think Mozilla does.

About the privacy policy, Mozilla has a "privacy tools" menu that lets you find out the privacy policy of a Web site; the default place that it looks is /privacy.html. (It also looks in a tag.)

So to go along with "Why is someone asking for /robots.txt?" soon we'll also have /privacy.html.
