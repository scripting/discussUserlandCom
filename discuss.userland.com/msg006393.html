Here's an article about a system for downloading music from a server to a kiosk which then records it on MiniDisc. The trial period for the first kiosks has just begun in Japan.

<http://www.connact.com/~eaw/minidisc/md_kiosk.html>

The benefits of this system are:

1. The media is much cheaper than the memory cards for a RIO.
2. You don't need a computer.
3. The sound really is CD quality.
