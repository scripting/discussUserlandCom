I've been playing with Dreamweaver and Frontier a bit, and for those of you who design Frontier templates in Dreamweaver, here's a present: FrontierDW.zip.

This archive contains two files, one XML file defining what a Frontier macro looks like to Dreamweaver, the other an icon (the cowskull "cowskull") which Dreamweaver uses as a visual placeholder for a macro. They go in Dreamweaver/Configuration/ThirdPartyTags/.

You can find out more here.
