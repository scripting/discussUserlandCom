I think there are two ways to go here.

First, create an entry (page) named "default" in your table of addresses for your subsites. Fill it in with your meta command to redirect to the page in your gdb (guest database).

Second, create an entry (page) named "default" in your table of addresses for your subsites. Make it a filespec to the file on your hard drive.
