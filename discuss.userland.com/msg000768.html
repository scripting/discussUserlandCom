Another key point, not to miss...

All messages posted to the discussion group are added to the search engine as they are entered into the system.

So if you search for ballmer, you'll find references to Brett Glass's musings on the time Steve Ballmer tried to hire him, and my suggestion, in Mozilla for the Wild-eyed Geeks that Ballmer join McNealy and Case onstage in February, both of which were discussion group postings.

http://search.userland.com/default?m=25&s=1&t=and&q=ballmer

This is important for people who have nuggets of information about Frontier that they want to make sure other people can find later. When you reach a conclusion, post a message somewhere in the discussion group and we'll have a handle to it.
