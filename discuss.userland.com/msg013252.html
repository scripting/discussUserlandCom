Interesting synchronicity!

We're having a discussion in the Frontier community about the connection between search engines and content management systems.

We'd love to see a powerful search engine that ties directly into our content management system. This way we could keep their index updated in real time, and furthermore, we could factor out the template information, providing them a flow of content.

Manila has a slot for this
