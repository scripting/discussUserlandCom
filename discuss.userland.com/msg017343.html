More seriously, I was, of course, engaging in hyperbole. That hyperbole is founded, however, upon a multi-decade-experientially-based belief that, in far too many cases, having the most awful consequences of whatever stupid action is taken occur is the only way that most people will learn. At least that was the intent of my comment, although I must acknowledge that I was fully aware of how the comment would be interpreted--that is, I accept some responsibility for your interpretation of my post. Besides, one of my goals was to initiate precisely this kind of dialog.
