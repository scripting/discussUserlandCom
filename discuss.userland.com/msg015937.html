As part of my Masters Degree in Computing Sciences, at Staffordshire University (UK), I am researching into the use of different operating systems as platforms for web servers and the different technologies that are used within web applications, such as CGI, Perl, ASP, XML. If you manage or develop your companies web server, or simply know about the technology involved, I would be extremely grateful if you could fill out a short survey to help with my research. The survey can be found at http://asp.soc.staffs.ac.uk/tj900498/aspsurvey.asp The survey will take literally 2 minutes to complete. None of the questions are compulsory but I would appreciate it if you could complete the survey to the best of your knowledge. If you consider any of the questions to be confidential then do not answer them. Thanks for your co-operation.
