Sidebar to the MacWEEK story

Author:Dave Winer
Posted:1/5/1999; 8:44:36 AM
Topic:Sidebar to the MacWEEK story
Msg #:1835
Prev/Next:1834 / 1836

An interesting sidebar for Mac developers who make tools that can plug into Frontier thru Apple Events.

Now we have a tangible way to show the editors of MacWEEK the benefits of the scriptability of the Mac OS and content development apps. In the past it's been difficult to explain the benefits in concrete terms. Now, if you can hook up to Frontier, it'll be easier to show them how your add-on or tool can make their site richer, or more timely, or connect to other resources on the net.

It goes without saying, I think, if MacWEEK understands your product, they'll be better-able to explain it to their readers.
