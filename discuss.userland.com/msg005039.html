templateSnippets

One of the strengths of Frontier has been the distinction between form and function, with the template/macro system.

But in mainResponder & htmlInterfaces a lot of the html-code is burried into the programming code with add().

templateSnippets tries to solve this, by making it possible to use templates for parts of the page.

templateSnippets, language versions, memepool type keywords and full thread structure can all be downloaded from http://www.xkom.se/frontier/techdemo/default.htm
