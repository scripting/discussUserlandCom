I'm sincerely interested in hearing what you think the bombs are accomplishing

The objective, as I understand it, is to destroy the infrastructure that keeps President Milosevic in power. An army is only as good as the infrastructure that supports it. At some point, it will come to the point that he will feel that the loss of his tools of power are not worth the cost. It is unfortunate that most of the ethnic cleansing is being done by paramilitary forces that can't be immediately stopped by bombing, but my belief is that they were going perform their cleansing act regardless of whether we bombed.

would the US have withdrawn from Vietnam when it did if the media hadn't been in the living rooms every night?

Maybe not, on the other hand, Vietnam went on for more than a decade, and we lost more than 50,000 dead. This conflict is less than 2 months old, and we have lost 2 dead.

B-2 mission is equivalent to 2500 WWII bomber missions seems ridiculous to me

I am only repeating what I heard a top level military officer state on national TV. He is probably correct. What he is saying is that since the B-2 can take out 16 targets in one mission, it would take 2500 missions in WWII to take out the same targets due to the inaccuracy of their bombs. The old way of carpet bombing certainly caused a lot more damage to the surrounding territory, it just couldn't take out the bridge or factory it was supposed to.
