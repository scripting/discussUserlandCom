Assuming we are talking about Emailer 2.0 and you have my semi-official glue installed, the following should work:

local (sig, siglist);
with objectModel, Emailer, eventInfo {
	siglist = get (signature[all].contents)};
for sig in siglist {
	msg (sig)}
