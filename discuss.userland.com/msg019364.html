I think the problem is this: If you let the player handle the streaming, RU will never really know where the player is in the queue, and if someone makes a change to the queue in RU, it will be next to impossible to get the RU queue and the player queue to sync up without flushing the player's queue.

And if you flush the player's queue, you still don't know what song it was playing at the time, so you don't know where to restart.
