Instructions

If you see a question in one of the mailing lists, and you can't find an answer in the search engine, reply to this message with a statement of the problem or question. Then answer that question, winnowing through replies here on the Discussion Group or on the lists till we get one page with a near-comprehensive answer.

  • Check the Search Engine first for an answer.
  • IF you can't find one, then reply to this message.
  • Be sure to change the title of your reply (helps for better searches).
  • Keep editing your message till you think you've gotten a comprehensive answer.
