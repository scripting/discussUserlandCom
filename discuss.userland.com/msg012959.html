I resist meta-ness whenever possible. It just pushes the problem down one level, and then that extra level throws some people out of the loop. What does the extra level do? No one is sure. So let chaos reign, because it already *is* reigning.

The Motley Fool guys, for example, have been waiting to put ticker symbols in their stories. I say we've waited long enough. I'm ready to support some more features in my aggregator.
