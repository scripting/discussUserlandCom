Something cool I also noticed through registering at joker.com: when I look up my domain at register.com, it displays it as taken and what my nameserver is and nothing else. My home address and phone number are not broadcast out to snail-mail spammers! This was always my big privacy beef with NSI, I never understood why personal domain registrants had to have their home address publicly available.
