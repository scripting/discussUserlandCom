Lighthouse Design made free two-user licenses for a number of their NEXTSTEP applications available a couple of years ago. Details and downloads are at <http://www.peak.org/next/apps/LighthouseDesign/>.

Also, as part of their Y2K plan, Apple has made an upgrade to a Y2K-compliant version of NEXTSTEP 3.3 free to anyone who owns an earlier copy of NEXTSTEP, or who owns a NeXT workstation. All you need is a serial number and you can order it. (Unfortunately, it's not available for download, but then again it's a few hundred megabytes...) Details are at <http://www.apple.com/enterprise/y2k/EnterpriseFAQ.html>.
