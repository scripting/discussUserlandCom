As silly as it may be, a lot of folks (including the iBook is a girl's toy set) are influenced by the way a computer looks. I guess I would fall into that category as well. Given two comporable systems, I would buy the one that looked nicer. At any rate, my employeer has said "no serious executive would put a shiny blue G3 on his desk. Though one sits on my desk, I must admit that a set of folks will not buy a G3 because of color. Well, now those types have an option. And what a good looking computer it is!

So, I do think varying the color is a major deal. It will move more desktops because they look great and, perhaps, won't scare away those scared of blue. And that helps my Apple stock!
