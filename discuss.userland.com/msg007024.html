DW said: >> BTW, the best way to debug a mainResponder request is to open mainResponder.respond and click on the Run button. A dialog will open asking for the URL. Paste it in.<<

I find it more helpful to place a breakpoint at the suspected problem script and press the "Debug" button in mainResponder.respond. Then press the "Follow" button. You'll soon find youself at the breakpoint with the option to "Lookup" the script params.
