It's fairly hidden...

Go to the Internet control panel.

Go to the Edit menu, choose 'User Mode...', then change to Advanced or Administration. A new Advanced tab will appear in the control panel.

Select the Helper Apps icon; select your preferred FTP client there.

As far as what FTP client to use, two popular ones are Fetch ( http://www.dartmouth.edu/pages/softdev/fetch.html ) and Anarchie ( http://www.stairways.com/anarchie/ ).
