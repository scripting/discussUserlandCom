DominoPower on UserLand 

Russ Lipton is a Lotus Notes developer. A few months ago he discovered Manila and then dove into Frontier and Radio UserLand.

Russ is a writer and a thinker. And he did something wonderful, he wrote an explanation of what we do at UserLand from the point of view of a Notes developer. His two pieces, linked below, are the top stories on DominoPower now.

DominoPower: How UserLand's Frontier can revolutionize content management.

DominoPower: A closer look at Manila and Radio UserLand.

I hope people in the Notes community read these articles. And a note to IBM people who read Scripting News -- we want to build bridges between our software and yours. We're working on a complete SOAP interface for Manila, we think it'll be the broadest SOAPable web app when it's ready (early December). We're a very small company focused on making the Web a great writing environment. We like working with big companies with large installed bases and marketing and distribution systems. We think we have good software, but we can't do it all.
