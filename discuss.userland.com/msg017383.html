I am in complete agreement on your comments about just in time searching. Much of the existing web pages have aged well beyond the brown banana stage and need active pruning. Will you be encouraging your content providers to use a content aging tag?

Even better it may open up real possibilities in effective web content creation. The next 18 months will see much of this a reality and improve the web for millions.
