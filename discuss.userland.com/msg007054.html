The Tcl-RPC mailing list is for people implementing XML-RPC in Tcl.

To subscribe to the list, send email to requests@userland.com with the subject:

   subscribe Tcl-RPC

To subscribe to the digest version, send email to requests@userland.com with the subject:

   subscribe digest Tcl-RPC

To post to the list once you've subscribed, send email to Tcl-RPC@userland.com.

To unsubscribe from the regular mail or digest version, send email to requests@userland.com with the subject:

   unsubscribe Tcl-RPC
