I bought my first PC clone from Debco Electronics almost 15 years ago. Since then, I've bought about 20 machines from them. All have come with OS installed, ready to turn on and get to work. If I have had trouble with a Debco machine, I've just hauled the box into their shop and they've gotten right on it. They've been most sensitive to my needs; if the machine in trouble was in a mission-critical role, they've given it top priority, and I've almost always been back in business within 6-12 hours. If anyone in the Cincinnati area is looking for a reliable clone maker, who stands behind the machines he builds, I'd recommend a call to Tom Case at Debco.

On the other hand, I've installed four NT server systems now, and last year I quit that business; nothing is worth that frustration.
