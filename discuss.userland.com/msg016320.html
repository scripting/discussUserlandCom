* I don't like bonds for the following reason:

For bonds to rise in value, interest rates will have to fall. Until very recently, we were at historic lows for interest rates. Stocks were doing very well at low interest rates. The same rising interest rate pressures that hurt stocks will hurt bonds. (although perhaps not at the same rate or timing)

In my world view, bonds have been a low return item that is currently correlated with stocks. I'd rather balance with cash/moneymarket (a low return item that is uncorrelated with the stock market).
