Apparently, someone at MIT is developing a new language for unifying web authoring, scripting, and development, called "Curl". Paul Howson stumbled over it and posted a pointer to their white paper on the ScriptMeridian list. It seemed like the kind of thing Dave and others here might be interested in. The white paper is at

http://www.cag.lcs.mit.edu/curl/wwwpaper.html

The idea excites me, but who's going to start using it, when the world is dominated by HTML browsers rather than Curl browsers?
