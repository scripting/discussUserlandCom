At LVT we were marching thru all the Flintstones characters for our codenames. I forget how this started. I think one of our competitors in those long-gone days had a programming language called Fred.

We looped back to LVT names briefly in 1997-8 because the HTTPization of Frontier was the realization of the goals of the original Betty, to have an outliner wired to a network.
