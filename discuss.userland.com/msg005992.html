For those that might be interested, I have a very early Proof of Concept RSS parser built for use by IE5 clients.

At the moment all it does is connect to the masterlist of files at my.userland.com and allow the choose which channel they would like to display.

I've posted it to http://xml.cameronmartin.com/rss/

Once I have an XML-RPC spec to work to this potentially will grow to be much more sophisticated -- I mainly wanted to build this Proof of Concept to validate that this type of system could be built in IE5.

Any chance of some doco? Work-in-progress is fine.
