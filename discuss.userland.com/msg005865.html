You should take a look at the DHTML Editing Component (for IE 4/5).

Full details available at:

* http://msdn.microsoft.com/workshop/Author/dhtml/edit/default.asp * http://msdn.microsoft.com/workshop/Author/dhtml/edit/ref/cncpt.asp

This ActiveX control provides full WYSIWYG HTML viewing AND editing. It is available as an SDK for IE4 and it is shipped as part of IE5.
