Didn't see this mentioned on Alphanumerica's Sullivan page, but for a double-click launch of NS PR1 on Mac you'll need to change the Sullivan desktop file's creator from MOZZ to MOSS.

'Course if you kept the Netscape alias the PR1 installer placed on your desktop, you can just drop-launch Sullivan onto it.
