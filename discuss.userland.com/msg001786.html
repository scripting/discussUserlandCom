What is Tonto?

Tonto is a small helper appliation for Frontier (currently for the Mac only). Register scripts with Frontier, and Tonto provides a floating window containing buttons (with icons, captions, or both) which activate those scripts when clicked.

This Release

This may be the final beta version of Tonto, it's quite solid. Final release is planned for the week of January 10, 1999.

http://www.macrobyteresources.com/scripting/frontier/tonto/

Tonto 1.0b3 has a number of improvements over 1.0b2, and answers every bug, and almost every feature request reported to date. Read more about it at the link, above!

Seth Dillingham, Macrobyte Resources
