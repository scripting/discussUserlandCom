A first..

Now on the Scripting News home page there's a box down the right edge containing the most recent dozen stories from Salon, Red Herring, Wired and Motley Fool.

This is an unusual kind of syndication because it's aggregated. I chose four channels out of the hundreds on My.UserLand because they're well-known brands that are identified with the Internet, and they're not widely syndicated, and I like to stay up to date on them.

It's an interesting and useful demo, but just one of many that are possible with the new power of syndicated content flow.
