Tip for Frontier webmasters

Topic:Tip for Frontier webmasters
Msg #:12686
Prev/Next:12685 / 12687

If you've been running a Frontier server for more than 1.5 years as I have on Nirvana, it pays to Save-A-Copy the following files:

  • config.root

  • members.root

  • discuss.root

And any other files that grow as your site grows.

The performance improvement is nothing less than stunning.

PS: The effect of save-copying config.root is the greatest. It's referenced several times on every hit.
