I've found another good way to use http://avantgo.scripting.com/ . I put it in the Page Holder on Microsoft's IE 4.5 for the Mac. It stays open in a little panel to the left, and I can click through the links and they appear in the main window on the right. http://www.scripting.com/ or http://www.scriptingnews.com/ are to big to fit well in that space.

This also works well in the Opera browser by using the "links from this window" and "links to this window" commands, and keeping http://avantgo.scripting.com/ as a little window to the side.
