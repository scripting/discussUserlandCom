I wouldn't say UserLand's brain dead for using MDI for Frontier 5 for Windows, but I will say I wish it didn't use it.

(I wonder what other Frontier users think. "Survey says"!)

Of course, MDI has it's place -- but my time with Frontier on the Mac skewed me in that direction -- I liked that mode of working better.

Visual Basic (Windows) lets you choose MDI or SDI -- I always choose SDI.

Dave, I can send you a screen shot of what VB looks like in MDI and SDI modes if you want to see how it does it.

I would also appreciate anyone who likes MDI telling me why -- real examples of how it can be useful/better... I'd like to understand how people on the other side of the fence rationalize this issue.
