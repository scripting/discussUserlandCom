What is a root-based url?

Actually, I don't know what is the formal name of it, but whenever I talked to other web people, they know what I meant.

When I said "root-based url", I meant a url specifying from the root down to the file, w/o the domain name. So,

http://discuss.userland.com/htmlInterfaces/discussionGroup/msgReader$

will become

/htmlInterfaces/discussionGroup/msgReader$

As far as I know, it wouldn't make any difference as long as the url come from the same domain.
