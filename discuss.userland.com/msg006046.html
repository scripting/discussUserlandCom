To subscribe to the Linux-Newbies mailing list, send email to requests@userland.com with the subject:

     subscribe Linux-Newbies

To subscribe to the digest version (where you get all the email in one long email once a day), send email to requests@userland.com with the subject:

     subscribe digest Linux-Newbies

To post to the mailing list, send email to linux-newbies@userland.com.

You must post from the same account you subscribed with.

To unsubscribe from the list, send email to requests@userland.com with the subject line:

     unsubscribe Linux-Newbies
