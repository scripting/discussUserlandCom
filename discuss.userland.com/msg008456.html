I would add:
  • If you're writing a robot to grab channels, make sure you provide a contact address in the user agent string so the owner of the site knows who to contact if there is a problem. Otherwise you risk being excluded from the site completely
  • Read up on the robots exclusion protocol and build that into your robot - it's easy to do with many packages.

In the interests of making this information public I run two robots, one active, the other under development, they identify themselves as:

rssmaker/1.0;webmaster@openjava.org
channelmirror/1.0;webmaster@startshere.net

I'd like other bot owners to do the same, I have a number of Frontier/6.0 entries in my logs that it would be nice to identify.
