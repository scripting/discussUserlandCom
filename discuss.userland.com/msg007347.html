Not really. We aren't passing the content off as our own and our policy is to always link the headlines back to the source site. If you are the owner of one of the sites we visit and you have any concerns or wish to comment on RSSMaker then you can email us at alchemy@openjava.org. You can also mail us if you have a site that you would like RSSMaker to index.
