I can hear my teacher's voice asking me "Dave, what is that you don't want to hear?" I'm laughing, I'm so weird.

You're not so weird. I have used a similar question: "What is it that you are most afraid of saying?" that has helped me make quite a few breakthroughs in trying to work through my own personal bullshit.

I would bet that saying what you are afraid of hearing is very powerful, reducing what was once a very large monster into a tiny little flea. And then you can recognize it, laugh at it, and leave it behind.

I wish I could remember to do that more often. Especially after a particularly bad weekend, which has me struggling to stay positive this morning.
