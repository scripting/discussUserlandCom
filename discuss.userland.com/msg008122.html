The Linux version of the My.UserLand.Com story list. (The URL is temporary.) It's a clone, the only way you can tell it's on Linux is the cute little penguin on the page.

The Linux interface has one neat feature: channel exclusion. If you click on a channel name, you'll see a channel screen with a button at the bottom for excluding stories. Click on the button, and you'll no longer see that channel on the Linux version of the site.
