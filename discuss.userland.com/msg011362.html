The error occurs whenever a page edit is attempted or any form data is posted.

There are three things:

1) Replace your current iisFrontier.dll with this version. You'll probably need to stop and re-start the IIS service to make the switch. This may fix your problem.

2) IIS, or the ISAPI interface, or the MFC ISAPI code, has a 48K POST limit. If you're POST is larger than 48K, Frontier will get a bad request. It's mentioned in the Known Bugs section on the docs page.

3) Frontier 6.1b2, the current beta, has vastly improved webserver performance. If you don't need IIS for legacy .asp pages or similar, then I'd run, not walk, to the Support site and try the latest beta and turn off IIS.
