I would be grateful if some of you guys would amble over to the URL below, take a good look and give some feedback on the overall concept and structure. Even better would be signing up as members and/or editors.

Criticizing the concept or the execution is acceptable .... ;-). Life is too short for this unless it is truly valuable.
