...the members box is definitely meant to catch your eye. The power is very limited without being a member...Most other sites not only put a box up, but they also put up two text boxes for the account name and password. We could not, no matter how we tried, justify that much space, so we settled for this design. It's eye-catchiness was very intentional.

It's my favorite part of the design. The member's login/logout is almost equivalent to an "on/off" button here. I think it works extremely well. I particularly like how you made it obvious without sacrificing too much space.
