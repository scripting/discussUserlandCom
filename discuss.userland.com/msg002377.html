Do the scripts in #filters need to be changed to use html.getPageTableAddress ()?

No. Since filter scripts take the address of the page table as a parameter, you don't need to change your filter scripts.

In other words: it's references to html.data.adrPageTable -- not references to all variables named adrPageTable -- that should be changed to html.getPageTableAddress () to take advantage of no-semaphores rendering.
