Envious of everyone else with their own sub-domains? wish you had more than just "default" in config.mainresponder.domains?

Well, you can! (Sort of, anyway.) config.mainresponder.domains won't give you "different" domains on different ports (pity!), but if you have a fixed IP address, you can have more than one domain.

Make one domain entry "my.domain.com", pointing to your site table, then make another domain entry "123.456.789" (your IP dotted.id, obviously), and point that somewhere else.

Viola! Two domains!

Told you it was messy.
