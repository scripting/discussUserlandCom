Q: What features would a world-class WWDC weblog contain?

Here's my take:

+ A great tagline: The Event Loop

+ A visual design that reflected the Mac OS--highly iconic

+ A "Your First WWDC" feature written by a veteran attendee

+ A pre-WWDC interview with Avie Tevanian or Steve Jobs (seriously). Q's submitted by members.

+ An "Images of WWDC" section with user-contributed images and captions. Have an award for the image that best captures the essence of the conference.

+ A "Heard on the Floor" feature

Best.

Robb Beal
