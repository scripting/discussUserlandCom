Net transaction taxation in California

Author:Jacob Levy
Posted:6/1/2000; 11:05:50 AM
Topic:Net transaction taxation in California
Msg #:17520
Prev/Next:17519 / 17521

According to this TechWeb article, the CA state assembly approved a bill that would add sales tax to all transactions for companies that have a physical presence in California.

If all fifty states do this, imagine the nightmare (or wet dream, depending on who you are) scenario that would ensue when companies try to keep track of exactly which local taxes need to be added to a transaction. Yikes.

Write your congress-critter and let them hear about where you stand on this issue.
