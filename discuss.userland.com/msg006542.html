Murray Rothbard was one of the so-called "Austrian economists". The Austrian School refers to a group of economists who made the case for laissez-faire capitalism at a time when capitalism was considered to be "discredited" by most academic economists.

Below are some relevant links (though perhaps a bit one-sided)
