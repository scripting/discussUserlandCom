We had an all-day offsite meeting today to plan out the next few months work towards the completion of Frontier 6. The plan is shaping up nicely. One of the things we wanted to do is to take a reading on how we're doing, given the recent release of Frontier 5.1.5.

So, if you've upgraded from 5.1.4 to 5.1.5, did we fix important bugs? Is it performing better? Are you using guest databases? What features are you looking for in Frontier 6 and beyond?
