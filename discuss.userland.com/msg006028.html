I checked out radio.yahoo.com but I think I still prefer Imagine Radio.

I can program my own station there, with artists played very often, rarely, or not at all, as I choose. Others can listen to my choices, or just read them.

I also like it because I can use Windows Media player or RealAudio. My home machine doesn't work so well with real audio, but WMP works fine.
