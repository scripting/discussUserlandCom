I don't have a problem with loading external links in an external window (in fact, I wish more people would do this - that's probably just my browsing habits, though).

My browser already has a feature which allows me to do this on a per link basis. I use it explicitly when that is what I want to do. If you target links to a new window by default, then you deny me the choice. The choice is very nice to have depending on the browser that I happen to be using and the level of brain damagedness of its history mechanism.
