FWIW, I'd advise sticking with the NGWS name. It's already fairly well-planted in our minds, and it doesn't have any unfortunate other meanings (as Win CE did), and as acronyms go, it's not too bad. For example "NT" which had a long run, used to stand for "New Technology" back when Microsoft was wanting to get out of bed with IBM on OS/2.
