From Seybold Bulletin Vol.5 No.26

After seeing Oracle enter the application service provider (ASP) business, it was only a matter of time before asset and content-management vendors began to take the database vendor's lead. Earlier this month Vignette announced that its partner, Breakaway, has begun hosting StoryServer as an ASP. Now the latest: Arbortext (http://www.arbortext.com) announced Tuesday its own ASP offering, called Extend. Extend sets its own precedent: It will be the first XML-based content-management software that you can deploy on an ASP basis.

See http://www.seyboldreports.com/Bulletin/about.html (Subscription req)

I question the first XML-based content-management software regarding all the Manila, Pike and Weblog offerings.
