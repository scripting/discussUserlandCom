Josh, instead of setting msgAdr^.body directly from the RPC handler, you should use mainResponder.discuss.setMessageText to make sure that other fields of the message table also get updated (e.g. the lastUpdate field and any cache data). This will also take care of running the body text thru mainResponder.neuterText.
