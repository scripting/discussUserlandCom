Here's a useful script for finding all the outlines in Frontier.root.

We list the address of each outline, followed by the number of lines in parens.

The larger outlines are the ones worth checking. They could be growing, and might get large and slow your server down. It happened to us.

Outlines to look out for: user.export.importLog, user.scheduler.log, anything in user.log.outlines.
